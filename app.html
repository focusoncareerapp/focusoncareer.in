About This App

Focus On Career is a structured learning platform specially designed for Electrical Assistant and all Gujarat electrical competitive examinations. The app provides syllabus-based preparation, mock tests, ebooks, video lectures and printed book ordering facility.

  • ✔ Electrical Assistant (GETCO, UGVCL, PGVCL, DGVCL, MGVCL)

  • ✔ Subject Wise Mock Tests
  • ✔ 3900+ MCQ Practice Questions
  • ✔ Video Lecture Facility
  • ✔ Ebook Study Material
  • ✔ Printed Book Order System
  • ✔ Fast WhatsApp & Email Support
  • ✔ Secure Payment Gateway
