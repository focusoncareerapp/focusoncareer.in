Website & Mobile Application Privacy Policy

1. Privacy Policy (English)

Focus On Career operates an educational website and mobile application providing mock tests, ebooks, video lectures and printed books. By using our platform, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

We collect basic details such as Name, Email, Mobile Number, City and Shipping Address (for printed books). We do not store card or banking details. Payments are processed securely via authorized payment gateways such as Razorpay or Google Play Billing (future integration).

We use your information only for account management, course activation, book delivery and service improvement. We never sell your personal data.

2. Payment Policy

All payments for digital courses, mock tests and ebooks are non-transferable. Access is activated only after successful payment confirmation.

Payment processing is handled by secure third-party gateways including Razorpay and Google compliant systems.

3. Refund Policy

Digital products (Courses, Ebooks, Mock Tests) are non-refundable once access is granted.

Printed books are eligible for return only in case of physical damage during delivery. Request must be made within 48 hours with proof. No refund for change of mind.

4. Shipping Policy (Printed Books)

Books are delivered via courier service. Delivery time: 3 working days (maximum 7 days). Cash on Delivery is not available.

5. Account Deletion Policy

Users can delete account directly from the App Profile section. Alternatively, request can be sent via Email or WhatsApp. Website requests are processed within 48 hours. After deletion, all personal data is permanently removed.

6. Educational Content Policy

All content is prepared by our team based on official syllabus and public exam patterns. We do not reproduce copyrighted books. Government public references may be used for academic purposes.

7. Data Security

We implement secure login, protected database and encrypted data transfer. However, no internet system is 100% secure.
